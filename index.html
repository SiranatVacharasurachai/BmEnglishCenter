Tenses & Verbs

Present Simple

Structure: Subject + base verb (add -s/-es for he/she/it)

Example: I work every day. She works on Mondays.

Past Simple

Structure: Subject + past verb (regular verbs add -ed)

Example: They visited the museum yesterday.

Present Continuous

Structure: Subject + am/is/are + verb-ing

Example: She is studying English now.

Present Perfect

Structure: Subject + have/has + past participle

Example: I have visited London twice.

Verbs
